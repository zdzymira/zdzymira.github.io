My best wishes

Drogi Czytelniku,

mam nadzieję, że przeczytasz te życzenia, chociaż jest już kilka dni po świętach Bożego Narodzenia, oraz mimo tego, że napisałem je po angielsku.
Niech Bóg będzie z Tobą.


Far away from city lights
- it was written in the stars -
there is born the Truth and Life;
Love, whose name is Jesus Christ

May Miriam from Nazareth,
chosen Mother by Her Son,
give the grace to comprehend
that you're His Beloved One.

May that humble, peaceful stream
bring what you're expecting to,
may you go encounter Him,
and may He recover you.


24.12.2021
